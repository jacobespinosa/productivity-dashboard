STREAKS:
   - A streak is about consistency. A goal is about achievement.
   - Days with at least one completed task. Very forgiving (Productivity)
   - Days reaching your focus goal. 2 hours/day (Time)
   - Days with every task completed. Harder achievement (Planning)
   - Days with at least one project session. Encourages progress on long-term projects. (Project Work)
   - Days all habits completed. If you later add recurring tasks (Habits) <-- Good Idea
   - Instead of every streak being "days in a row," include a Best Streak alongside the current one.
        Current 🔥 8 days
        Best 🏆 31 days

   3 Best Ones: (Rest go to ANALYTICS PAGE)
      - Days with every task completed. Finishing everything you planned (Execution)
      - Days reaching a daily focus goal. Spending enough focused time (consistency)
      - Longest Streak (Personal Record)
